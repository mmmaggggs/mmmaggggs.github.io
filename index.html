When it comes to television, I find I watch a little bit less than the typical person. However, sometimes I reach points where I obsessively watch a show or go through a phase of certain movies and I'll be watching something every day. I try to keep my television intake to a minimum so that it is a nice little treat when I take time to watch something.

Books are my weakness. I become very wrapped up in a good book and ignore anything else I have going on. My challenge with books is trying to manage my time correctly and to not drop everything for a good book. But if I'm not dropping everything for it, is it really a good book? That's why there's a book sitting on my nightstand untouched...I'm too busy to read.
